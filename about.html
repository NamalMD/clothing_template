OUR PHILOSOPHY

Kinetic was founded on the belief that clothing should be an extension of the body in motion. We reject static design. Our pieces are built for the fast-paced, ever-changing urban environment.

We source the finest materials from around the globe and assemble them with meticulous attention to detail. Every seam, every stitch, every print is calculated.
